Appearance Notes

Tall and lithe, but well built with lean muscle. Long legs, with proto-hoof like feet. Angular features with a small, pointed chin, and a small nose with a bump in the middle of the bridge. Broad, antler-like horns with additional spines on his jaw, chin, ankles, and elbows (kept filed down). Wild, scraggly hair, partially matted around the back where it was once tied up. Yellowed, shark-like teeth; dark purple gums; and a black forked tongue. Bright purple scleras with lime green irises and square pupils. Dark, pronounced, bags under his eyes.

Voice Notes

Higher than you may expect, but still noteably masculine, with a raspy quality. Talks very quickly and incoherrently. Commonly exhales and makes grumbling/growling/etc noises to emphasize his speech. Sometimes will speak telepathically and verbally at the same time, often saying slightly different things. When calm or particularily present, he speaks slower, and with a softness not otherwise heard.

Backstory

A tiefling who was abandoned as a small child, somehow found his way into the catacombs of a large military fortress, and was adopted and raised by the ghosts who haunt there. He has no memory of his life before them, and nobody is quite sure how he ended up there to begin with.

He had to stay hidden to avoid being kicked out, leaving the catacombs only at night with one of his parents (usually Fen) to steal food, and never leaving the fortress itself. His childhood was pretty normal despite his situation, with the only real event being his magic manifesting... there were many frantic 2 AM trips to the fortress' library. Although he was content enough with just the company of his family, as he got older, they started to worry that he might need some better socialization - so when he turned twenty, they pushed him to leave and go interact with living people.

He gets homesick sometimes, and he's had a lot of trouble making friends - but he's doing his best to be brave and independent, even though it's a new thing for him. He's alone for about 6 months before the start of his DnD campaign, time which he's mostly spent travelling and trying to adjust to being outside and around people.

Personality Overview

Shy
Spook has very little experience with strangers, and is already shy by nature. He struggles to approach people he doesn't know.
Quiet
Even at his most comfortable, Spook both moves and speaks quietly. He's easily overstimulated and gets non-verbal in busy situations, so he's sometimes unable to talk at all.
Cautious
Spook does not make decisions without some kind of planning, and in dangerous situations, he'll always take the safest viable option - regardless of anything else.
Kind
Spook has a big heart, and is kind to anyone who doesn't give him reason to be otherwise.
Curious
Despite his cautious nature, Spook absolutely loves seeing and learning about new things, taking in whatever he can while staying out of trouble.
Honest
Spook is an open book - he's not one for secrets, and he's not one for hiding his feelings or thoughts. Sometimes he can be harsh or blunt, but this is rarely intentional.
Focused
Spook is incredibly good at staying on task. If he has his mind set on an objective, he'll puruse it diligently until it's completed, and is very hard to distract.
Protective
The few people Spook considers family are very important to him, and he does not take well to them being upset or harmed in any way. He will not hesitate to intervene.
Volatile
Although he's generally very calm and withdrawn, his sheltered upbringing shielded him from a lot of stronger emotions. If he's overwhelmed, his emotions can get the better of him.

Trivia

LOVES bugs, especially beetles. Loves to identify and document any he finds, something he's kept doing to some degree even in his current state.
Despite the disrepair of his clothing, his two rings and necklace are in noticeably better condition.
Has trouble controlling his psionic abilities, and tends to bleed telepathy when around people, often talking telepathically while talking out loud or broadcasting his thoughts unintentionally.
Prefers to sleep on his back, but can't do so comfortably without something shoved under his horns and neck to support his head better. Lying down in general is a bit awkward.
He's farsighted, and needs glasses to read. He still has his old reading glasses, although they're quite scratched now.
Keeps his elbow spines filed - they used to knock things off of his desk, and filing them down has become a strong habit.

Loosestrife's mom. She's a well respected hunter and trapper in their small town, mainly catching small game and selling pelts for a living. She's friends with everyone, and while she's not one for gossiping, news tends to make its way to her anyway.

She's upbeat and energetic, and always tries to put a positive spin on things. She and Loosestrife share an interest in nature and the outdoors; however, he certainly doesn't share his mother's interest in hunting, much preferring to observe and document wildlife than hurt it. She initially tried to take him on hunting trips with her, but when it was clear it wasn't for him, she settled for short camping trips instead.

When Loosestrife didn't come back, she went out searching for him - but eventually, she got too far into dangerous territory to continue, and had to turn back for her own safety.

Loosestrife's dad. He's a blacksmith, forging weapons, armour, tools, and anything else you can really imagine. Does a bit of farrier work too.

He's very stoic and calm, rarely showing anything other than irritation on his face, and speaking with a dry, level tone. However, this isn't an accurate representation of his personality - he's a diligent, considerate, and devoted person, putting his best effort into all of his passions. He was initially a bit disappointed that his son had absolutely zero interest in smith work, but kept those feelings to himself, and did his best to encourage his son's actual interests. The two of them don't have a lot in common, but Casmeros has always been a reliable rock, and Loosestrife knows his dad loves him no matter how different they are.

He carried on after Loosestrife's disappearance for the sake of his family, burying himself into his work and doing a rather good job of not letting his own hurt show.
